Influential Professionals

Kevin Feige

Kevin Feige is an American film and television producer who is the president of Marvel Studios and the chief creative officer of Marvel Entertainment. He is the mastermind behind the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU), an interconnected universe of superhero films that has captivated audiences worldwide. Some of his most successful films include The Avengers, Black Panther, and Spider-Man: Far From Home among several others. Feige is known for his visionary leadership, collaborative approach, and keen understanding of what makes a successful superhero film. He has won numerous awards, including the Motion Picture Showman of the Year award from the ICG Publicists Guild and the Academy Award for Best Picture for producing Black Panther.

Chan Phei Yong

Chan Phei Yong is a Malaysian YouTuber, radio host, actor, and entrepreneur. He was born on February 4, 1994, in Tanah Merah, Kelantan, Malaysia. He first gained recognition as a radio DJ after winning the "MY FM Be My Star" DJ competition in 2015. Later, he transitioned into content creation, launching his own YouTube channel in 2017, where he produces vlogs, short films, and product reviews. Chan Phei Yong's YouTube channel is a vibrant mix of entertainment, lifestyle, and music content. He has amassed a significant following, with over 823K subscribers. His videos range from vlogs, interviews, and comedic skits to music collaborations with other Malaysian artists. The videos, variety shows, and commercials he produced are all of high quality. His videos are well-liked by the Chinese people in Malaysia, and the filming angles, editing techniques, and rhythm of the videos are excellent. In addition, the high-quality commercials he shoots are also very popular among advertisers and audiences, so many advertisers like to ask him to shoot commercials. Beyond his digital presence, Chan Phei Yong has also ventured into acting, appearing in films and television shows. Additionally, he owns a fashion brand called "Step8ight". His career reflects his versatility, as he continuously explores new creative avenues.
